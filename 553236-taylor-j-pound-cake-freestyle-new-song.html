Pound Cake (Freestyle)

Posted by Kelle Repass on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Rising Saint Paul, Minnesota emcee Taylor J has put his own spin on Drake, Jay Z and Boi-1da's "Pound Cake", from the former's latest album Nothing Was The Same. 

The last we heard from Taylor was "Michael Jordan", an unconventional but dope ode to the NBA great. His upcoming Soul Food mixtape is slated to drop on February 9th, and will be hosted by hip-hop historian, cultural ambassador and brand strategist Shaheem Reid, who's slowly but surely showing him the ropes in the music industry. Stay tuned. 

(This freestyle was brought to you by DJ Advance and TJ's own imprint, Scenious Society.)
