Tess Daly and Claudia Winkleman dazzle in new Strictly Come Dancing pictures

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 17, 2024

    Tess Daly and Claudia Winkleman have posed for photos to promote the latest series of and the two presenters look stunning!

    In the snaps, the duo wear an assortment of dresses, with Tess opting for a bright yellow gown to complement her long blonde locks in a close-up shot, a Charleston-style black dress, a chic midi black pencil dress, and a show-stopping embellished gown with cut out panels and a sheer skirt.

    Tess and Claudia looked stunning in the promo snaps

    Meanwhile, Claudia wore a white one-shoulder Grecian-style gown as she posed with Tess, another one-shouldered black number with one long sleeve, and a floor-length black gown with a purple panelled bodice.

    The pair have already revealed how "excited" they are for the upcoming season, and revealed that they now know every contestant who are now slowly being revealed. In a video posted on the BBC's Twitter site, Claudia said: "There's one name that made me scream."

    Tess then joked: "When you found out you screamed out loud. You got very excited. She screams a lot."

    Tess wore a chic yellow gown for the photo shoot

    Nine of the contestants in the line-up have been revealed so far, with former MP Ed Balls, Will Young and Daisy Lowe among those who will be competing in the popular dance competition. It was revealed only earlier today that presenter Naga Munchetty will be the ninth contestant in the show, and the presenter opened up about appearing in the show, saying: "I'm going to give it my very best and enjoy what is bound to be a great adventure.

    "I am equally excited, nervous and scared – I can't wait to start training. I was so surprised to be asked to take part – I never thought I'd have the courage to say yes, but it would be simply silly to turn down the chance to learn to dance with a professional."
