    Oliver Hudson was raised by one of Hollywood's most beloved acting pairs and off-screen couples, Goldie Hawn and Kurt Russell, and he is doing his best to try to make them proud.

    Though Bill Hudson is the star and his sister's biological father, the siblings have always considered Kurt to be their "Pa," and they also haven't shied away from opening up about their relationship with both of their dads.

    Bill wasn't very present during the Hudsons' childhood, having separated from Goldie in 1980, though as the actor has grown older, he has been candid about trying to mend their estranged relationship.

    Speaking with , Oliver discussed his role in , and struggling with the similarities he sees between his upbringing and that of his character.

    He explained: "His father was a criminal — was a bad person — and he was raised by his uncle, who was a firefighter and a war hero."

    He added that: "It really sort of mirrors my life a little bit — not in the exact situation, but my relationship with my father, and Kurt coming into my life and raising me."
