Fan Asks Karen Jarrett Who Was Better In Bed Between Kurt Angle &amp; Jeff Jarrett During Q&amp;A

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Karen Jarrett is now face in AEW, and she’s appearing on-screen alongside her real-life husband Jeff Jarrett. Double J was not the only WWE Hall of Famer that Karen has been romantically linked to in the past, and one fan in London had to bring it up in a very public fashion.

One fan in the UK had a chance to ask a question, and he had zero chill about it. This fan, who was clutching a clear cup in one hand, asked Karen Jarrett, “who’s better in bed, Jeff or Kurt Angle?”

The crowd was floored by the question, and Karen Jarrett was not happy. She asked this fan to repeat his question. They did not give the fan the microphone again. Then Karen went on to answer that question.

“Billy? What’s his name? Billy, who am I sitting next to right now? That should answer your question.”

The crowd laughed a bit and put Karen Jarrett over by how she handled the situation. It didn’t excuse this bold fan’s inappropriate question. It seemed that this fan, presumably named Billy, had a lot of fun at the Q&A.
