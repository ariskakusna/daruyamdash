LA Knight Threatens to Knock Roman Reigns Teeth Down His Throat Post-SmackDown

Posted by Tandra Barner on Thursday, August 8, 2024

LA Knight has been on a roll over the past few months, as he singlehandedly became one of the most over Superstars in WWE. He finally began his feud with Roman Reigns on Smackdown last week and Knight continues to be popular even now. He also made a bold promise regarding his match with Roman Reigns next month after Smackdown went off the air.

LA Knight began his feud with Roman Reigns last week on Smackdown as John Cena endorsed Knight as Reigns’ next challenger. Naturally, fans are excited for how WWE has been booking him so far. It was then confirmed Knight will challenge Reigns at Crown Jewel on November 4th.

After Friday Night Smackdown went off the air, Jimmy Uso interfered in Jey Uso vs Drew McIntyre and beat down his brother before LA Knight made the save. Knight would then cut a promo in the ring with Jey Uso present, where he sent a message to the Bloodline, especially Rman reigns. Knight promised to knock Reigns’ teeth down his throat at Crown Jewel.

”You know your family’s a real pain in the ass right? We have Solo, Jimmy and what do they call him, the Tribal Chief, right? How about this, when I get to Saudi Arabia, I’m gonna knock every one of his big, shapeless sized Tribal Chief teeth down his throat. You’re gonna be looking at the new WWE Undisputed Universal Champion, because I made it that way. “

We will have to see how the company will continue to book Roman Reigns and LA Knight’s feud in the coming weeks, as fans are more than happy for Knight’s growth.
