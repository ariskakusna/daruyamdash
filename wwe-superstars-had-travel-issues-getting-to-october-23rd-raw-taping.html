WWE Superstars Had Travel Issues Getting To October 23rd RAW Taping

Posted by Billy Koelling on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

WWE has a full schedule for their talent, and they need to make dates all around the country. That being said, it isn’t always an easy trek making it to each city.

WWE RAW brought their show to Texas this week, and they had a full show for the event. Ringside News also provided comprehensive coverage of the show.

PW Insider reports that WWE talents had some trouble getting to RAW this week. It wasn’t noted which talents had these issues, but it appears that there was a problem.

“There were quite a few WWE talents with flight issues heading to Raw yesterday.”

We have not heard anything about WWE having to change any plans for RAW this week due to travel issues. We will update this story, and produce a new article, if that news comes to light.
