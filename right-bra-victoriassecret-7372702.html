How to Find the Right Bra for Your Outfit

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

Finding a bra that goes with your outfit can be one of the most difficult parts of getting dressed. Sometimes, the bras you have just won't cut it for the look you’re trying to achieve. Maybe, you want to wear a sheer top but don’t have a lace bra for underneath, or maybe you found the perfect strapless dress but know that you’ll still need some support.

Not all bras are created equally—aka, you can’t exactly wear a T-shirt bra with said strapless dress—but Victoria’s Secret has options to complement every outfit. Keep reading to learn about five bras we love and how to style them. 

Dream Angels Wicked Unlined Lace Balconette Bra 

Sheer and mesh tops are taking over this spring, and the Dream Angels Wicked Unlined Lace Balconette Bra is the perfect companion piece. All-over chantilly lace keeps it sexy while bows on the straps keep it sweet. Plus, the neutral colorways are simple enough to be worn under something sheer. With a less embellished top, wear it in Claret Red or Pink Berry to add a pop of color. The underwire balconette style and innovative sling also provide support and a push-up lift without padding. 

Dream Angels Wicked Unlined Lace Balconette Bra $44.95

Very Sexy Unlined Floral Embroidered Demi Bra

For a playful and seriously sexy look, try the Very Sexy Unlined Floral Embroidered Demi Bra. Made with sheer mesh and precision-cut satin with embroidery, this bra is perfect when you’re looking to make a statement. Style it with a cropped white shirt to allow the color to shine through, or a low-cut top to show off the plunge front silhouette. 

Very Sexy Unlined Floral Embroidered Demi Bra $69.95

Very Sexy Bombshell Add-2-Cups Push-Up Strapless Bra

Introducing the ultimate strapless bra. The Very Sexy Bombshell Add-2-Cups Push-Up Strapless Bra is supportive, stylish, and functional, so you can feel comfortable all day. No-slip sides ensure that the bra stays put no matter how much you sweat or move around, and the plunge front allows you to wear it under shirts with deep necklines. As the name implies, this bra gives you some serious lift, adding two cup sizes. Wear it with both removable and adjustable straps if you’re sporting a tank top, one strap for one-shoulder looks, or strapless for all other outfits. 

Very Sexy Bombshell Add-2-Cups Push-Up Strapless Bra $54.95

Very Sexy Push-Up Bra 

Victoria’s Secret’s No. 1 push-up bra, the Very Sexy Push-Up Bra, is a best seller for a reason. Seamless cups provide a medium lift—up to one cup size—with no uncomfortable pinching, and a smooth microfiber Infinity Edge lining ensures that the bra remains nearly invisible under T-shirts and thin fabrics. Fully adjustable straps allow you to wear it either classic or crossback, and four size settings and a double row of hook-and-eye closures mean you can easily achieve your best fit. While it can be worn with a cute going-out outfit, it's also comfortable enough to wear under your favorite lounge set for a night in. 

Very Sexy Push-Up Bra $49.95

Very Sexy Satin & Lace Shine Strap Push-Up Bra

Similar to the Very Sexy Push-Up Bra, the Very Sexy Satin & Lace Shine Strap Push-Up Bra is the perfect blend of comfort and lift. This iteration includes slightly more padding for an additional boost. It also features rhinestone straps for extra shine and delicate lace detailing. We love that the plunge neckline helps keep it hidden under V-necks for a nearly invisible look that can add up to one full cup size. 

Very Sexy Satin & Lace Shine Strap Push-Up Bra $69.95

Remember, you can always find your perfect size in-person with the help of an associate at Victoria’s Secret stores, or online with their handy Bra Fit Quiz.
