Naomi Campbell to star in new TV show based on hip-hop empire

Posted by Tandra Barner on Sunday, August 11, 2024

    Supermodel Naomi Campbell has been confirmed to be taking a lead role in new TV show,The show will be directed by acclaimed director Lee Daniels and follow a hip-hop empire, headed by Lucious Lyon (Terrence Howard), the head of a record label at the centre of the series.
