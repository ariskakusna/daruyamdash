Who is AJ McLean's estranged wife, Rochelle DeAnna?

Rochelle DeAnna McLean was born on October 7, 1981, in Phoenix, Arizona.

She is a hairstylist and makeup artist, as well as an occasional model and dancer.

AJ and Rochelle met in 2001 at Choreographer's Carnival where she was a Halloween dancer.

The two reconnected years later and started dating in 2009, later tying the knot in December 2011.

AJ proposed 10 months into the relationship and had popped the question at his birthday celebration in Las Vegas.

Rochelle often stays out of the spotlight, but was featured in the Backstreet Boys' film specials, A Very Backstreet Holiday and Backstreet Boys: No Place.

Are AJ McLean and Rochelle DeAnna getting divorced?

On January 1, 2024, both AJ and Rochelle posted a joint statement on Instagram, announcing their divorce.

They wrote: "While we have hoped for reconciliation we have decided to officially end our marriage."

"Our focus now is moving forward in the healthiest way possible with friendship and co-parenting our girls at the forefront of this next chapter," they continued in the statement.

"We appreciate your kindness, respect, and privacy at this time."

The news comes after the couple told TMZ in March 2023 that they would be separating temporarily to work on themselves.

At the time, they said: "We have mutually decided to separate temporarily to work on ourselves, and on our marriage with the hope of building a stronger future."

"The plan is to come back together and continue to nurture our love for one another, and our family," they continued.

"We ask for respect and privacy at this time."
