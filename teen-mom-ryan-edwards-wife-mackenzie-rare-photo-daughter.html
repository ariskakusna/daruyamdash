Teen Mom alum Ryan Edwards' wife Mackenzie shares rare photo of dad with daughter Stella, 2, after f

Posted by Kelle Repass on Sunday, August 4, 2024

TEEN Mom alum Ryan Edwards' wife, Mackenzie, has shared a rare photo of the dad with his young daughter Stella, after their family was fired from the MTV show.

Mackenzie, 25, shared a snapshot of her husband and his brand new motorcycle on her Instagram. 

Ryan, 34, wore a red shirt and had his black cap on backward.

The Teen Mom OG alum planted a kiss on the little one, who was sitting on the Harley as well. 

Stella had on a green outfit as her long blonde hair was tied into a ponytail. 

Mackenzie wrote: "Just a dad, his daughter, and his Harley," along with a back heart emoji.

In an Instagram Story, Stella sat on the Harley by herself. 

Stella flashed a smile at the camera as she kicked up her leg. 

She held her arms out as if she was pretending to ride the motorcycle. 

The former reality star, her hubby Ryan, and his family were fired from the popular series amid the drama with his ex Maci Bookout, 30, and husband Taylor McKinney, 32.

Back in March 2021, the family was axed after his parents, Jen and Larry, had a nasty fight with Ryan’s baby mama Maci and her hubby Taylor during the Teen Mom OG reunion special.

THE FIGHT

During the reunion special, Jen and Larry were sitting on a couch opposite Maci and Taylor.

They yelled back and forth about Ryan's first born, son Bentley, as Taylor angrily pointed his finger at Larry and Jen.

Taylor argued: “Ryan has never been there for him, but y’all put it on Bentley!”

Ryan’s father pushed off the couch and got closer to Taylor.

He yelled: “You know what!”

Ryan jumped off the couch towards Larry as both wives held them back.

Jen and Maci grabbed their husband’s arms and tried to pull them back and make them sit down again.

Along with kids Jagger and Stella, Mackenzie also shares son Hudson, eight, with her ex-husband, Zachary Stephens.

Ryan, 34, shares his 13-year-old son Bentley with Maci, who now has two kids Jayde, six, and Maverick, five, with Taylor.

'BETTER PAY ME'

Back in September 2021, Ryan exclusively told The Sun that he was demanding the network to pay him for his appearance after being on the reality show's trailer.

He told The Sun: “They just better pay me."

He explained: “MTV just stands for a lot of things that my wife and I don’t support, politics and respecting people’s rights, that kind of stuff. 

“I just don’t agree with them on a lot of stuff and I didn’t want to work for some place that was like that anymore, they were just way too far out there for me."
