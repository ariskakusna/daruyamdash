ASICS promo codes - $20 OFF in July 2024

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 29, 2024

FAQs

Where can I find ASICS free shipping?

There are two ways to secure ASICS free shipping. The first is to place an order that totals at least $125. These orders will be eligible for free ground shipping. The second is to sign up for the OneASICS program. This is free to sign up for and will grant you free ground shipping on all of your orders with a minimum fee of $50. Expedited shipping options are available but will incur a fee. From time to time, we also have an ASICS code that can help you claim free shipping. 

How often does ASICS have sales?

You can find sales at ASICS off and on throughout the year. Many times, sales will fall around holidays. If you don’t have time to wait, check out the clearance page on the ASICS website. This section is dedicated to showcasing all of the products currently available at a discount with some prices lower than $50. If the product you love isn't in the sale, you can save on it with our available ASICS discount codes. 

Does ASICS offer student discounts?

Students can benefit from a 15% ASICS discount code thanks to their student program. If you want to get a student promo code on your next purchase, visit the student discount page on the ASICS website for more details about how to verify your student status and secure your coupon code.

Does ASICS have a newsletter?

ASICS offers a newsletter for customers on the ASICS website. This is a great way to stay informed of upcoming sales, ASICS coupons, and more. Members can sometimes receive exclusive offers from the newsletter, as well. It’s free to sign up for the newsletter and you can unsubscribe at any time. To join, enter your email address into the form at the bottom of the ASICS website.

What is ASICS return policy?

ASICS offers their customers 90 days from the date of purchase to return any unwanted products. Unfortunately, there isn't an ASICS free returns option and each return will incur a cost of $4.99. 

Hints and tips

Newsletter Benefits: Do you love ASICS sneakers and apparel? Sign up for ASICS emails and you can stay updated on the latest products, sales, and brand information. You can sometimes get emails about exclusive sales and online discounts from ASICS with the newsletter. ASICS also send new shoppers a first-order discount every so often so keep an eye out. 

Sale Savings: You can shop discounts of up to 50% off before with the ASICS sale page. Simply go to their website and click the ‘Sale’ from the main menu bar. You can also hover over the ‘Sale’ for specific promotions. The best times to shop the sale are during the ASICS annual sales and Black Friday sales. Check this page closer to the time, as we update our discounts regularly. 

Heroes Discounts: ASICS does its best to give to first responders, military, and medical professionals. You’ll find ASICS coupons of up to 25% off on their website, with a valid work ID. To access your discount, you will need to verify your work status through your SheerID account.

Teacher Discounts: Much like first responders, military, and medical professionals, teachers can save on their future orders too. When you verify your employee status with SheerID, you will receive an ASICS 15% off code to use on the next order you place. 

Free Shipping Offers: If you would like free shipping, you can get this on orders of over $125. You could also sign up for OneAsics - the ASICS loyalty program - for free and get free shipping on each order over $50. All you need to do is go to the OneAsics page on their website and type your preferred email address to sign up. Check this Marie Claire page for a free shipping promo code too, if needed.

Join OneAsics: Sign up for the loyalty program OneAsics to get 10% off your first order. You can also get free shipping on orders over $50 and a flat-rate of $15 for all of their 2-day shipping methods. ASICS will send you exclusive products and sales. Sign up with your email address for free on their website or by clicking the relevant code on our page.
