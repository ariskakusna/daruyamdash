Dream Doll Musical Artist Biography, Age, Family and Career Achievements

Posted by Tandra Barner on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Tabatha Robinson, better known professionally as Dream Doll is an American model, rapper, television/radio personality, singer, and songwriter. She is currently under contract with the Gwinin Entertainment label. The artist is biracial from the Bronx in New York, where she was born and raised. She shot to fame after her appearance on the Oxygen reality show Bad Girls Club, and it was the show that propelled her to prominence. In 2015, she was arrested in Florida and charged with prostitution.

Dream Doll has a cult following on Instagram, her page has over 2.2 million followers. Read more facts about his career and personal life below.

Dream Doll Biography, Age

Tabatha Robinson was born into a middle class family in the Bronx, New York. Her date of birth is recorded as February 28, 1992. Robinson was born to an African American mother and a Caucasian father. She lived with her family until she was 19 when she decided to leave the family home. It wasn’t too long after she graduated from high school.

Because her family could not sponsor her to attend college, Robinson had to work in others to raise money for the college. She worked as a bartender at a strip club called Starlet’s Gentlemen’s Club located in Queens. She collected enough to enable her to begin her studies and, as she entered, she continued to work to pay her bills. Ultimately, she earned a degree in business management.

Leaving home to start a new life was not easy for young Tabatha Robinson, life was difficult and she had to struggle to survive. This led her to engage in the oldest profession in the world. It was while residing in Miami Beach, Florida that she was arrested and charged with prostitution. She was charged as Tabatha Unique Gibbson, leading people to believe that may have been her real birth name after all.

The controversies never really stop whenever Robinson’s name is mentioned. A few years ago, a leaked sex tape was released. In the video, she is seen giving oral sex to an unknown man.

Like many celebrities today, Dream Doll isn’t shy about showcasing her body on social media. On her Instagram page, she likes to joke her followers with sexy pictures of herself wearing sexy dresses and mostly in her underwear.

Achievements of the Dream Doll

Looking at when and how she started, it’s amazing how her career has grown in just a few years. Tabatha Robinson took it upon herself to become famous as she believed it was the only way out of her past mediocre life.

She started by modeling on Instagram, building followers. When she surpassed over one hundred thousand followers on Instagram, she was offered the opportunity to participate in Bad Girls Club in 2016. She accepted the offer and became one of the roommates or the sixteenth season of the reality show. She entered the series as Tabatha Robinson, but later adopted the alias “Dream Doll.” After appearing in two episodes of the show, she was kicked out for assaulting and harassing a roommate. However, his short time on the show was enough to elevate him to stardom.

She started her rap career in May 2017, when she signed with DJ Self-owned Gwinn Entertainment. She dropped her debut single titled Everything Is Nice on May 12, 2017. She released her first extended play in September of the same year and titled it Plastic Life. The EP is in the top 20 of the iTunes Hip-Hop charts.

From 2017 to 2018, she was cast in the eighth season of VH1. Love and Hip Hop: New York. She starred on the show as a supporting cast and appeared in over ten episodes. Although the musician has yet to win any awards for her hard work, she is generating good wealth, enough to secure her future and that of her family. Dream Doll net worth is estimated at $400k.
