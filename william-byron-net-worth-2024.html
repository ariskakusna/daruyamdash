Early Life and Career Beginnings

William Byron was born on November 29, 1997, in Charlotte, North Carolina. His passion for racing was ignited at a young age when he watched a race at Martinsville Speedway. He began his racing career in iRacing, a realistic online motorsports simulation game, before transitioning to real-world racing.

Professional Racing Career

Byron’s professional racing career took off when he joined Kyle Busch Motorsports in 2015. He quickly made a name for himself, winning the NASCAR Camping World Truck Series Rookie of the Year in 2016. His success continued as he moved up to the NASCAR Cup Series, where he currently races for Hendrick Motorsports.
