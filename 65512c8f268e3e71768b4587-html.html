OnlyFans model and Michigan fan celebrates victory in a tiny blue bikini

Posted by Billy Koelling on Friday, August 16, 2024

Mia Sorety is one of the biggest fans of the Michigan Wolverines, who have been in the eye of the storm following the suspension of head coach Jim Harbaugh.

This weekend, the head coach was able to make the trip to Penn State, the team's home field, but the attention was diverted to his team's number one fan.

After the game, Mia sent a message to the non-supporters who were predicting an imminent Wolverines loss to the Nittany Lions. She posted a video posing in a blue bikini and a crop top with a "Michigan" print.

The model and influencer has shared on her social networks her unconditional support for the University of Michigan team for a long time.

She also posted a photo confirming her presence at the stadium that drove her fans crazy... and her "haters" too.

Mia Sorety is a professional model and OnlyFans sensation

Sorety has experience in the modelling world, having worked on photo shoots, commercials, and other campaigns with several prestigious jewelry, clothing and lifestyle brands.

She has also been able to participate in various fashion catwalks.

On social media she continues to promote some brands, although she is now one of the most popular OnlyFans models in the United States.

Some of her content on the platform is free, although she also has a VIP section that costs just under 10 dollars a month. At the moment there is an offer available: four dollars for a 30-day-subscription.
