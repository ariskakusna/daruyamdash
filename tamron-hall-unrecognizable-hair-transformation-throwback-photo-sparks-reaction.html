Tamron Hall rocks volume-heavy long hair transformation in unrecognizable throwback photo

Posted by Tandra Barner on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

The Tamron Hall Show star went on a trip down memory lane this week

    Tamron Hall is gearing up for Halloween, and is more than excited about dressing up on October 31. 

    Ahead of time, star took to Instagram to share an epic throwback photo of herself dressed up back in 2019, where she transformed into none other than Dianne Carroll. 

    The TV favorite looked almost identical to the actress, even down to her iconic hairstyle. Tamron's hair was visibly longer and styled in tight curls that was styled in a sleek side-do. 

    She rocked purple eyeshadow and a full red lipstick, and wore a stylish white dress and feather boa, just like Dianne was wearing in the picture Tamron had replicated. 

    Fan comments at the time included: "Split image!" and "You've nailed it!" as well as "You look amazing." 

    Tamron had re-posted the 2019 throwback this week on her Instagram Stories, alongside the caption: "Throwback to THEE look you guys favored the most." 

    Tamron's had a busy week, as on Monday night she hosted the star-studded The Angel Ball gala, which raised money for Gabrielle's Angel Foundation for Cancer Research. 

    Among the celebrity guests were Tina Knowles and Mary J. Blige, who performed at the event, as well as Robin Thicke. 

    Tamron was the perfect host for the event, and has had her own successful talk show since 2019. It has proved such a success, that it's picked up the Daytime Emmy Award for Outstanding Informative Talk Show Host both in 2020 and 2022. 

    Away from work, Tamron loves nothing more than spending time with her family. The star has been incredibly open about her personal life over the years, and detailed the emotional journey she took towards motherhood, resulting in the arrival of her little boy Moses, now four, back in 2019. 

    Moses was conceived by IVF when Tamron was 48, and she was more than delighted. In March 2019, the TV personality shared on Instagram that she was expecting her first child, posting a beautiful photo of herself showcasing her baby bump, while explaining in the caption that she was 32 weeks pregnant. 

    She wrote: "I've wanted to share this news for many months and now finally my doctor has said I am in a safe place, at 32 weeks, to share my joy with y'all. So, it’s clear a daytime talk show isn’t the only thing I've been trying to produce! 

    "There have been many tears, but today I embrace the smiles. My husband Steven and I are beyond excited!" 

    Tamron and her husband Steven Greener, a music exec, had only just got married earlier that year, making the news even more special.
