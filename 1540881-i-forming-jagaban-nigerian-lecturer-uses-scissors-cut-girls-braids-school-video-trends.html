I Was Forming Jagaban: Nigerian Lecturer Uses Scissors to Cut Girls Braids in School, Video

Posted by Tandra Barner on Sunday, August 11, 2024
  • A young Nigerian girl has shared a video of her lecturer cutting her long braids with scissors at school
  • In the video, the lecturer seemed so focused as he held the scissors firmly and removed the student's braids
  • Social media users have shared their thoughts about the video, with many condemning the lecturer's actions

Mixed reactions have been trailing a viral video of a lecturer cutting a female student's braids.

The trending video showed the Benson Idahosa University lecturer carrying out the act with total concentration.

The student, who has been identified as @real_peace_seven07 on TikTok, shared the video on her page with the caption:

"When you know the kind of school you are going and you are forming jagaban. I love my school so much."
