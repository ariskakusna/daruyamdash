Dil Raju Getting Trolled For Removing Wife's Name

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, August 23, 2024

After his wife Anitha expired in 2017 due to a cardiac arrest, leading producer Dil Raju remained unmarried for years. But due to insistence from family, he married air hostess Tejaswini (aka Vygha Reddy) in May 2020, during the lockdown. But then, here comes an unexpected trolling for him.

Actually Dil Raju’s production house, Sri Venkateswara Creations, is always preceded by the line “Smt Anitha Presents”. Ever since the producer made his debut with Dil, he has his wife’s name on the top of the banner’s name in the title cards. And for the recently released teaser of “V”, which was uploaded on YouTube six months ago, this name is present.

But the twist is that the latest trailer of “V” that was uploaded by Amazon Prime two days ago, bears no name of ‘Smt Anitha’ though it has “Sri Venkateswara Creations”. Many netizens are trolling Dil Raju for removing Anitha’s name because he got married now. But some say that Raju did the right thing as he should give equal importance to his new wife as well rather reeling around Anitha’s thoughts and name all the time.

Whatever it may be, Dil Raju is back in the news on social media after the producer took a long break from all film related activities.
