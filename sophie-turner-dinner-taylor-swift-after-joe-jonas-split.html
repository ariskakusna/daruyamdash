Sophie Turner Gets Dinner With Taylor Swift in First Outing Since Joe Jonas Split

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, August 8, 2024

Sophie Turner has officially joined Taylor Swift’s girl squad.

The actor reunited with the singer last night for what was Turner’s first public outing since splitting from Joe Jonas.

She and Swift—a fellow Joe Jonas ex—grabbed dinner at New York City hotspot Via Carota, and were seen interlocking arms as they walked around the city.

For the girls’ night, Turner looked cool in a glittering silver ab-baring top and baggy gray pants. She wrapped a black cardigan around her waist and finished the laid-back party outfit with white sneakers, a black leather shoulder bag, a chunky gold chain necklace with a lock on it, and gold hoop earrings. Turner kept her hair in a messy updo and wore silver eye shadow and a subtle pink lip.

Swift also kept it casual, opting for a comfy red knit dress by Reformation, a blue denim trench coat from EB Denim, and brown leather croc-embossed knee-high boots from Reformation. She accessorized with various gold necklaces and earrings, and wore her hair in a ponytail. As for glam, she went minimal with a muted red lip.

BACKGRID

Until last night, Turner has stayed off the radar since announcing her breakup from Jonas earlier this month. On September 6, the Jonas Brothers singer and guitarist shared a statement on Instagram that read: “After four wonderful years of marriage we have mutually decided to amicably end our marriage. There are many speculative narratives as to why but, truly this is a united decision and we sincerely hope that everyone can respect our wishes for privacy for us and our children.”

Turner and Jonas, who married in 2019, share two daughters.

Swift briefly dated Jonas back in 2009, but he allegedly ended that relationship with a quick phone call. Though there doesn’t seem to be any more bad blood between them, Swift went on to write her 2021 hit “Mr. Perfectly Fine” about Jonas.
