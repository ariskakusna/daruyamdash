Biodun Okeowo aka Omoborty speaks about her prominent curves, says they cannot be hidden

Posted by Patria Henriques on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Nollywood actress Biodun Okeowo popularly known as Omoborty has gained prominence in the movie industry not only because of her talent on screen but because of her curvy nature.

The actress who is mostly known for her roles in Yoruba movies recently opened up about how she has been dealing with the attention that comes with her curves in an interview with The Sun.

Fielding a question about having an embarrassing moment during her childhood because of her curves, the actress made it clear she did not really have any awkward moments as such.

She, however, gave a brief narration about a man that used to refer to her as ‘Iya Iyabo’ back when she used to reside in Kaduna state.

So, that made me conscious of my shape; as skinny as I was then, my curves couldn’t be hidden."
