Kendall Jenner Ignores Winter in a Fluorescent Yellow Micro Bikini

Posted by Tandra Barner on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Who needs winter when you can have white sand beaches and endless sunshine? Kendall Jenner seemed to get that memo, judging from the recent streak of sun-kissed bikini photos she’s been sharing on Instagram.

The model embarked on a New Year’s trip to the Bahamas with her cohort of famous friends, including BFF Hailey Bieber and younger sister Kylie Jenner. During the tropical getaway, Kendall got to indulge in a rotation of enviable, summery swimsuits.

In photos posted to her Instagram Story yesterday, the 818 founder looked cool in a dark maroon micro bikini from Amsterdam label Deparel. The stretchy knit set featured ribbed strips of ruffled fabric in a bright fluorescent yellow, covering both the bikini top and drawstring bottoms. Jenner accessorized the beachy look sparingly, opting to add only a pair of black sunglasses, gold huggie earrings, and a few chunky silver earrings.

She reminisced over the trip, writing over the pictures, “But mentally I’m still on a beach … take me back.”

Jenner shared a look at a different Deparel microkini in another Instagram post earlier this week. In those photos, she poses against a dramatic sunset while in a similar swimsuit—though this one was a citrusy orange with cream-colored ribbing. She accessorized with gold earrings and a few silver rings then, too.

Her tropical vacation included more than just bikinis, of course. For a more glammed-up evening look, the model stunned in a completely see-through cream mesh gown from supermodel Elsa Hosk’s fashion brand, Helsa. It featured ruffle trim, a diamond cutout on the abdomen, ruched detailing at the center of the waistline, and a high-low skirt.
