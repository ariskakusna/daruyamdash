Target's Affordable Fragrance Line Has Designer Dupes of Chanel, Billie Eilish &amp; More

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 10, 2024

If you purchase an independently reviewed product or service through a link on our website, SheKnows may receive an affiliate commission.

Want to smell bougie on a budget? Then you’ve gotta get your hands on a bottle of Fine’ry fragrance by Target. The much buzzed-about brand has churned out a lineup of beautifully packaged perfumes and body mists that are dupes for some of the most popular (and pricey) scents on the market, from YSL Black Opium and Chanel Chance to coveted celeb launches like Eilish by Billie Eilish and Cloud by Ariana Grande.

They’re so identical, the ‘perfume girlies’ of TikTok have made them go viral — but Fine’ry fragrances have one key difference from their designer counterparts: they’re a fraction of the price. I’m talking just $13 for a five-ounce body mist and $28 for a two-ounce bottle of perfume. You can save hundreds — and honestly, who’s going to notice these scents are dupes? If you’re a perfume pro, you’re bound to find your signature scent in the Fine’ry collection. And if you’re new to the high-end fragrance game, no sweat: TikTokers are actually breaking down each formulation so you can find your signature scent. If you want to smell fresh, says one, grab Fine’ry Before the Rainbow; it’s a dead-on dupe for Dolce & Gabana Light Blue. Prefer a musky smell a la Cloud by Ariana Grande? Fine’ry I’m a Muse hits all the right notes.

Some of the scents are so similar, Fine’ry didn’t even bother creating an air of mystery when they named them. The dupe for $230 Le labo Santal 33, for instance? That one is Fine’ry Jungle Santal — just $28 for a bottle that’s slightly bigger. One TikToker called it “unlocking luxury without breaking the bank.”

TikToker Olivaolfactory, who dubs herself ‘Your Fragrance Fairy Godmother,” points out that Fine’ry fragrances “don’t list what they’re supposed to be dupes of.” Furthermore, people who aren’t up to speed on their expensive perfumes wouldn’t even know what the designer predecessors smell like in the first place. So she created an entire TikTok demystifying the Fine’ry line. And we’ve listed out nine Fine’ry scents — from $13 to $28 each — making a major splash at Target. Dupe for: Chanel Chance ($135)

Fine’ry Flower Bed

“Clean floral bubble bath meets candied green apple” is how TikToker Oliviaolfactory described this Chanel dupe, while Target calls it “carefree joy in a field of blooming flowers.” Like all Fine’ry perfumes, it’s vegan and cruelty-free. Get a proper two-ounce bottle for under $30.

Dupe for: Tom Ford Soleil Blanc ($295)

Fine’ry Sunphoria

If you want to smell like vacation, this paraben- and pthalate-free Tom Ford dupe is the move. Fine’ry Sunphoria smells “like a creamy coconut meets floral sunscreen” with the experience of being “warmly kissed by the golden sun.” It has hints of orange blossom and amber wood.

Dupe for: Yves Saint Laurent Black Opium ($130)

Fine’ry Midnight Cafe

If you want to smell like a fragrance legend, Fine’ry Midnight Cafe gives Black Opium in a major way but for $100 less. It has hints of jasmine and patchouli and gives “the intoxicating mystery of midnight.”

Dupe for: Eilish by Billie Eilish

Fine’ry Sweet on the Outside

The pop star’s popular fragrance has notes of vanilla, soft spices, and cocoa, according to her site, and the aptly named Sweet on the Outside follows through with hints of Madagascar vanilla, cacao wood and smoked vetiver. It’s “a hypnotic and delicious veil of smoky vanilla,” and we’re here for it.

Dupe for: Tom Ford Lost Cherry ($80) or Kayali Lovefest Burning Cherry ($89)

Fine’ry Not Another Cherry

For just $13, you can get a five-ounce bottle of body mist that smells like the popular Tom Ford Lost Cherry — or Kayali Lovefest Burning Cherry, depending on who you ask. Either way, you will smell like “that last decadent bite of an amaretto-soaked cherry.” Spray it right after you get out of the shower.

Dupe for: Le labo Santal 33 ($99)

Fine’ry Jungle Santal

The original has Australian sandalwood, cedarwood as well as “spicy, leathery and musky notes,” and Fine’ry Jungle Santal has the same woody appeal, with “papyrus Wood, Indian cardamom, black oolong tea” and “the lush and steamy air of an exotic, sultry rain forest.” That trip to Bali may not be happening any time soon, but you can smell like it is.

Dupe for: Baccarat Rouge 540 ($325) or Cloud by Ariana Grande ($65)

Fine’ry I’m a Musk

With hints of musk, cotton blossom and sandalwood, I’m a Musk gives cult fave Baccarat Rouge 540 a run for its money. It may make you feel like you’re “blissfully floating through a daydream,” which sounds a lot like the experience you’d get from the fragrance’s other doppelganger, Cloud by Ariana Grande.

Dupe for: Sundazed Byredo ($205) or Prada Candy ($135)

Fine’ry Magnetic Candy

One “like cotton candy” with notes of musk and citrus, the other “an oriental gourmand scent,” these two much-loved fragrances find their rival in Magnetic Candy, a “nostalgic escape into a neon candy fantasy” with sugared violet, pink pomelo, cotton candy. It’s safe to say this one’s citrusy.

Dupe for: Jo Malone Wood Sage & Sea Salt ($152) or Dolce & Gabbana Light Blue ($96)

Fine’ry Before the Rainbow

Nope, not over the rainbow. This Fine’ry fragrance is Before the Rainbow, and it’s “the refreshing calmness after the rain” with the smell of salty air, bergamot and tree moss. It’s a dupe for both Dolce & Gabbana Light Blue
