Virgil van Dijk becomes first defender to win Goal 50 best player award

Posted by Kelle Repass on Thursday, August 15, 2024

- Virgil van Dijk and Megan Rapinoe have been crowned winners of the Goal 50 best players

- The Liverpool star is now the first defender ever to win the award

- Van Dijk has been impressive this season for Liverpool who are topping the Premier League standings

Liverpool superstar Virgil Van Dijk together with USA female player Megan Rapinoe have been named the winners of the Goal 50 awards.

Virgil Van Dijk is the first defender ever to win the award following his superb performances for Liverpool since the past two seasons.

34-year-old American Megan Rapinoe was recognized for the big award because of her incredible goalscoring form at the Women's World Cup.

Van Dijk defeated the duo of Lionel Messi and Liverpool teammate Mohamed Salah to win the award for the first time.
