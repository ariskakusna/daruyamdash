9 Red Coat Outfits to Wear Right Now

Posted by Billy Koelling on Friday, August 23, 2024

Since the fall, red has been having a moment like never before—and has gotten a boost thanks to the Kanas City Chiefs' #1 fan Taylor Swift. Swift's whirlwind romance with Chiefs star Travis Kelce is still dominating headlines as the pop powerhouse continues to make appearances at NFL games across the country and the Chiefs inch closer to Super Bowl LVIII. Showing support for her man, she's been sporting Chiefs (whose colors are red and gold) gear left and right.

It got us thinking: If red coats have Tay's stamp of approval, they're bound to have one from other style stars, too. So we went ahead and rounded up red coat outfits to wear right now.

Big and Fuzzy

Take a page out of Taylor's book with a fuzzy coat so soft and cozy it's almost like being wrapped up in your favorite blanket—but you can leave the house wearing it.

A Vibrant Puffer

A puffer jacket is a versatile cold-weather staple. This one features a double collar for added warmth and is the perfect shade of vibrant red.

Leather Layers

Red and leather are a match made in heaven. We love how a black turtleneck dress adds extra drama to the oversized lapels on this coat.

An Oversized Set

Everyone needs an oversized coat in their winter wardrobe. This suede style makes a statement no matter where you're headed.

A Chic Cape

This look is a masterclass in nailing multiple trends at once thanks to its rosette shoulders and bright cherry-red hue. Not to mention, it's the perfect layering piece.

A Patent Anorak

If you don't have an anorak, take Jessica Simpson's 'fit as your sign to get one. They're stylish and windproof so you can brave all the elements while looking chic.

Red-on-Red

It's common knowledge by now that a sleek set of satin or silk pajamas can absolutely be dressed up. Go all-in on the red trend with a coat, feather set, and pumps to match.

Long With Leggings

Chances are you already have a trusty pair of black leggings on rotation during the cold winter months. Take them up a notch by throwing on a red duster coat and pointed toe heels—instantly elevated!

A Blazer

Add a pop of color with an oversized red blazer. Go the sexy route and pair it with matching trousers and a bralette or throw it over an LBD and tights for a night out.
