Is nudity legal in New Jersey?

New Jersey has ambiguous laws surrounding nudity within the state, allowing governing bodies of each municipality to determine if it is allowed within their jurisdiction.

However, a statewide law prohibits exposing oneself in a lewd or offensive act to a non-consenting individual.

Every municipality in New Jersey is permitted to make, amend, repeal, and enforce ordinances regulating nudity.
