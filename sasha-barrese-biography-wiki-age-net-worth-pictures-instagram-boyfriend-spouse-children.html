Early Life and Education

Sasha Barrese, now 43 years old, was born on April 24, 1981, in Maui, Hawaii, to Katherine, a single mother and actress.

She moved to Paris, France, with her mother when she was four years old, where her mother worked as a model.

She spent her childhood traveling between France and the U.S. and learned to speak French fluently.

She started acting as a child, appearing with her mother in the films Homer and Eddie (1989) and Jezebel’s Kiss (1990).

She attended several private schools, including Tabor Academy in Massachusetts, where she graduated in 1999. She also studied at Ojai Valley School in California and The Buckley School in Los Angeles.

Career

Sasha Barrese had a successful career as an actress, spanning over two decades. She had small roles in films such as American Pie (1999), Hellraiser: Inferno (2000), and Legally Blonde (2001), and TV shows such as Just Shoot Me!, LAX, and Supernatural.

She gained fame for playing Tracy, the bride of Doug (Justin Bartha), in The Hangover (2009), The Hangover Part II (2011), and The Hangover Part III (2013).

She also appeared in the horror film Let Me In (2010), based on the Swedish novel and film Let the Right One In. She quit acting after The Hangover Part III and pursued a career as a professional poker player.

She announced her plans to become a poker player in May 2014 and competed in several tournaments, including the World Series of Poker in Las Vegas. She has won over $100,000 in poker earnings so far.
