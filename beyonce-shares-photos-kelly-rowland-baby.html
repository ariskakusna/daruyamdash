Beyonc shares first photo of Kelly Rowland's newborn son: 'Baby Ty, I love you so much'

Posted by Kelle Repass on Sunday, August 11, 2024

    Beyoncé has shared the first photo of Kelly Rowland's son Titan Jewell Witherspoon. Two days after Kelly welcomed her precious baby boy into the world, her best friend Beyoncé took to Instagram to celebrate his arrival.

    View post on Instagram 

    Beyoncé has shared the first photo of Kelly Rowland's newborn son Titan

    "There's no better feeling than holding my beautiful nephew," the 33-year-old – herself a mum to two-year-old Blue Ivy - wrote, "I thank God for the honour of witnessing my sister's journey into motherhood. "I've always dreamt of our little ones growing up together. I'm so thankful for our bond. Baby Ty, I love you so much. Congrats to my Spoons."

    View post on Instagram 

    Beyoncé also shared a glamorous pregnancy shot of Kelly 

    In addition to the touching message, Beyoncé shared a black and white photo of Titan's tiny hands. She also shared a striking pregnancy shot of Kelly, 33, showing the singer dressed in a glamorous gown and holding her baby bump. Kelly and her husband Tim Witherspoon welcomed Titan, their first child, on Tuesday 4 November. The little boy was born at 1.30pm and weighed 7.5 lbs.

    'Sisters' Beyoncé and Kelly Rowland

    The following day, Beyoncé and Kelly's Destiny's Child bandmate Michelle Williams said the new mummy was "doing very well". "Kelly's going to be an amazing mum," she told . "She's always been great with children. She loves babysitting, she loved buying stuff for kids. She has such a big heart. She's a giver and a nurturing person and, really, that's what motherhood is all about."
